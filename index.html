I'm Angelo and I'm a systems analyst / developer based in London, United Kingdom. I have a passion for web design and love to create for web and mobile devices.

CARE ABOUT THE CUSTOMER.

While Software Developers intend to consider people first, the result is a massive abstraction away from real problems people feel on a daily basis. An abstraction away from simply building something you would love to see in the world, and the hope that others desire the same. If we remove the term “users” from our vocabulary, replacing it with “customers”(the more specific “buyers” & “sellers”), we can psychologically set a higher bar on the level of service we provide as we do not want to risk losing their attention or business. The word “user” (often how the entire technology industry describes its customers) abstracts the actual individual. While it might be convenient, no one wants to be thought of as a “user” (or “consumer” for that matter). I certainly don’t, And I wouldn’t consider my mum a “user” either, she is my mum.

INVEST IN IDEAS.

Barack Obama once said "When we fail to invest in research and innovation, we fail to invest in the future". Innovation is crucial to the success of any organisation. In my line of work, innovation for a public service is designed on helping citizens feel as though their concerns are being heard and noticed. For a corporation, the aim may be creating methods and systems that help save money over time. While these aims may seem unconnected, both can help to increase the quality of life: in government, for the citizens, and in the corporation, for the employees.

BE PASSIONATE.

As a well known educational entrepreneur once said (Anthony J.D'Angelo), if you “Develop a passion for learning”, you “will never cease to grow”. Making sure you squeeze every last drop out of life you possibly can by always learning, always growing, always experiencing new things will allow you to improve upon yourself and your surroundings.
